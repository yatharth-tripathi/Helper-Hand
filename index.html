Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

To book a service, navigate to the Services section, choose the service you need, and click on "View Service Details". From there, you can fill out the booking form with your preferred date and time.

What if I need to cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any charge. To cancel, go to your user profile and find the booking in your list of services. Click on "Cancel Booking" and follow the prompts.

Are your helpers vetted?

Yes, all our helpers undergo a thorough background check and vetting process before being allowed to offer services on our platform. We prioritize your safety and the quality of service.
