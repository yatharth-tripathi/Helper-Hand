Trust & Safety

At Helper Hand, your safety and peace of mind are our top priorities. Here's how we ensure a secure experience:

Rigorous Vetting Process

All our helpers undergo thorough background checks and interviews before joining our platform.

Verified Identities

We verify the identities of both helpers and clients to ensure transparency and trust.

Secure Payments

All transactions are processed through our secure payment system to protect your financial information.

Insurance Coverage

Our services are backed by insurance to provide additional protection and peace of mind.

User Ratings and Reviews

After each service, users can rate and review their helpers. This system helps maintain high-quality service and allows you to make informed decisions when choosing a helper.
